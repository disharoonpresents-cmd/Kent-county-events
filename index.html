About This Project

The community calendar for Kent County and the Eastern Shore, gathered so you don't have to hunt.

The Goings-on is a volunteer-run event aggregator for Kent County, Maryland, built for anyone tired of the digital scavenger hunt. We gather upcoming events from local venues, arts organizations, and community calendars into a single, streamlined listing. It's updated regularly so you can stay in the loop without checking a dozen different websites or staying tethered to Facebook.

How it works

Our AI handles the heavy lifting, extracting event data from the sources below to keep things in chronological order. We also hand-fill the gaps with local events that don't appear elsewhere.

Have an event?

If you're hosting something or know of an event that isn't on our radar yet, don't let it be a secret. Use the submission form below to suggest it for the listing. Submissions are reviewed before being added.

This project is community-built and independent — not affiliated with any county organization.

About & Sources

The community calendar for Kent County and the Eastern Shore, gathered so you don't have to hunt.

The Goings-on is an event aggregator for Kent County, Maryland, built for anyone tired of never knowing what is going on locally. We gather upcoming events from local venues, arts organizations, and community calendars into a single, streamlined listing. It's updated regularly so you can stay in the loop without checking a dozen different websites.

Have an event?

If you're hosting something or know of an event that isn't on our radar yet, don't let it be a secret. Use the submission form below to suggest it for the listing. Submissions are reviewed before being added.

This project is volunteer-run and independent. We are not affiliated with any county organization. Designed by Disharoon Studios and powered by AI.
